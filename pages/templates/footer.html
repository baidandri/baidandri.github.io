About WHO


Windmill Hill Observatory houses a Meade 0.4 m LX200GPS telescope. The Telescope features a 0.4 m diameter primary mirror with a 4064mm focal length and f/10 focal ratio. It was relocated from University of Warwick's La Palma Observatory to the Windmill Hill Observatory in 2022.
